CHOCOLATE ALMOND CROISSANT ROLLS


PREP TIME: 25 MINUTES

COOK TIME: 15 MINUTES

TOTAL TIME: 40 MINUTES

YIELDS: 12


INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pack (12 rolls) King’s Hawaiian sweet rolls
  • 1/2 cup milk or semi-sweet chocolate chips or chopped chocolate chunks
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • powdered sugar, for dusting on top

ALMOND SIMPLE SYRUP

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract

ALMOND FILLING

  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/3 cup + 1 tablespoon granulated white sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 large egg

    INSTRUCTIONS

    1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish or sheet tray with parchment paper.

    h4> MAKE THE ALMOND SIMPLE SYRUP:

    2. In a small saucepan, combine water, sugar, and almond extract. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar fully dissolves. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.

    MAKE THE ALMOND FILLING:

    3. In a mixing bowl, add almond flour, granulated sugar, a pinch of salt, unsalted butter, one egg, vanilla extract, and almond extract. Beat everything together until smooth and creamy. Reserve about ¼ cup of the filling to use for dolloping on top later.

    PREPARE AND BAKE THE ROLLS:

    4. Without pulling them apart, slice the entire pack of King’s Hawaiian rolls in half horizontally like a sandwich. Place the bottom half in your prepared dish.

    5. Brush the inside of both the bottom and top halves (cut sides only) with the almond simple syrup.

    6. Spread the majority of the almond filling evenly over the bottom half of the rolls. Sprinkle chocolate chips or chopped chocolate all over the filling.

    7. Place the top half of the rolls back on. Spoon little dollops of the reserved almond filling across the top of the rolls. Press sliced almonds into the filling.

    8. Bake uncovered for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the tops are golden and the filling on top is set and slightly crisped.

    9. Let cool slightly, dust with powdered sugar, and pull apart to serve warm and gooey!

HONEY BUTTER TOAST


PREP TIME: 10 MINUTES

COOK TIME: 20 MINUTES

TOTAL TIME: 30 MINUTES

YIELDS: 6



INGREDIENTS

  • 6 or 12 slices white sandwich bread (use 12 if the bread is thin and stack them in pairs; use 6 if the slices are thick)
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    INSTRUCTIONS

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.

    2. If you're using thin white sandwich bread, use 12 slices and stack two on top of each other to make 6 thicker pieces. Just make sure the bread isn’t too soft—something like fresh Wonder Bread won’t hold its shape well. If you're using a whole loaf, slice it into pieces about 1.75 to 2 inches thick.

    3. Slice off all the crusts before moving on.

    4. In a microwave-safe bowl, mix together the melted unsalted butter, honey, light brown sugar, vanilla, and a pinch of salt. Microwave for 30 seconds to 1 minute, then whisk really well until it turns into a smooth, glossy syrup.

    5. Coat each piece of bread generously on all sides with the syrup and place them on a nonstick baking pan—no parchment needed.

    6. Bake for 15 minutes, then carefully flip each one and bake for another 5 minutes until golden and sticky all over.

    7. Let them cool for about 10 minutes before serving. They’ll feel soft right out of the oven but will crisp up beautifully as they cool slightly. Enjoy them warm!

DUBAI CHOCOLATE CHEESECAKE BARS


PREP TIME: 45 MINUTES

COOK TIME: 45 MINUTES

TOTAL TIME: 6 HOURS, INCLUDES CHILLING TIME

YIELDS: 12 BARS


INGREDIENTS

CRUST

  • 1 2/3 cup graham cracker crumbs, very fine
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 tablespoons granulated white sugar

CHEESECAKE FILLING

  • 24 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

PISTACHIO KUNAFA

  • 2 cups kataifi (shredded phyllo)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1 1/4 cup pistachio cream

CHOCOLATE GANACHE

  • 1 cup milk or semi-sweet chocolate
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

    INSTRUCTIONS

    CRUST

    1. Line an 8x8-inch square pan with parchment paper.

    2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated white sugar. Mix until well combined.

    3. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan, creating an even crust. Set aside.

    CHEESECAKE FILLING

    4. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).

    5. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and white sugar together until smooth and creamy using an electric mixer.

    6. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.

    7.Stir in the heavy cream, sour cream, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix until smooth and fully incorporated.

    8. Pour the cheesecake filling into the prepared crust and smooth the top with a spatula.

    9. Bake for 35 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is slightly jiggly.

    10.Let the cheesecake cool on the counter for 1 hour, then transfer it to the refrigerator to chill for at least 4 hours, or overnight.

    PISTACHIO KUNAFA

    11. Heat vegetable oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.

    12. Once hot, fry the kataifi until golden and crispy, stirring occasionally. Remove from the pan and set aside to drain on paper towels.

    13. Once the kataifi has cooled, mix it with pistachio cream until fully combined. Set aside.

    CHOCOLATE GANACHE

    14. In a microwave-safe bowl, heat the heavy cream in the microwave until it just begins to simmer (about 30-45 seconds).

    15. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for 10 minutes.

    16. After 10 minutes, stir the mixture until smooth and fully melted.

    ASSEMBLY

    17. Once the cheesecake has chilled, top it with the pistachio kunafa mixture, spreading it evenly across the top.

    18. Drizzle the chocolate ganache over the pistachio kunafa and cheesecake layer.

    19. Refrigerate the cheesecake for an additional 30 minutes to allow the ganache to set before slicing and serving.
